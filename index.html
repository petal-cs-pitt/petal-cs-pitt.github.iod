PETAL

Pitt EducaTional And Language technology lab

Welcome!

The educational and language technology lab at the University of Pittsburgh consists of a diverse group of researchers passionate about computational models for texual and spoken language, primarily motivated by educational applications. Our current work focuses on the areas of argument mining, summarization, spoken dialog, writing tutor etc.
